My UX Skills

User Research

I have a passion for learning about our users. I always question the way things are designed and try to put myself in the users shoes when designing a layout I'm working on

Wireframing and Prototyping

Wireframing is one of my favorite parts of the design process. Getting to combine all the work I've done so far and putting the work into a design is such a rewarding experience.

User Empathy

As much as I like Wireframing and prototyping, I think user empathy might be what I'm best at. I've always questioned the experience consumers have and it really helps me empathize with them during the design process
